THIS THE SIGN FOR:
A BIKING PATH

ISGA x TRAFFIC SIGNS

The literal meaning of this sign is, "Bicycles are required to drive on this path". So when you see this sign, you need to bike on the path where the sign is located.


Previous

Explanation

Let's start with what you can observe by just looking at the sign. The sign says "fietspad", which means biking path. Knowing this makes it easier to understand the signs meaning.

Now for the shape and color, as you can see the sign is rectangular and blue. The rectangle and blue combination in road signs means there is a requirement, the vehicle (or pedestrian) portrayed on the sign is required to drive on the road or path where the sign is placed.

So, the sign is rectangular, blue and says "fietspad". This means that when you see this sign you need to bike on the path where te sign is located. Beware, you'll only be passed by other bikers.

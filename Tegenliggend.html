THIS THE SIGN FOR:
ONCOMING TRAFFIC

ISGA x TRAFFIC SIGNS

The literal meaning of this sign is, "Beware, on this road/path you can encounter oncoming traffic". So when you see this sign, you need to be careful when overtaking someone. Since there can be oncoming traffic on the oposite lane.

Explanation

Let's start with what you can observe by just looking at the sign. The sign portrayes two arrows pointing in oposite directions, which means you can expect oncoming traffic. Knowing this makes it easier to understand the signs meaning.

Now for the shape and color, as you can see the sign is rectangular and white. The rectangle and white combination in road signs means the sign gives guidance, the sign guides the driver that sees it by letting them know they can expect something (in this case oncoming traffic).

So, the sign is rectangular, white and portrayes two arrows pointing in oposite directions. This means that from where the sign is located, the driver can expect oncoming traffic. When you see this sign, often hanging under another sign, you need to beware when biking next to- or overtaking someone. This way you can easily prevent a collision of any kind.

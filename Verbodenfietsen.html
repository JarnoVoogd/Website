THIS THE SIGN FOR:
CLOSED TO BICYCLES

ISGA x TRAFFIC SIGNS

The literal meaning of this sign is, "This path/road is closed to bicycles". So when you see this sign, know that it is forbidden to bike there and try to find another biking path.


Previous

Explanation

Let's start with what you can observe by just looking at the sign, it's supposed to be a bycicle. Knowing this makes it easier to understand the signs meaning.

Now for the shape and color, as you can see the sign is round and has a red circle around the edge. The round and red circle combination in road signs means there is a restriction, the vehicle (or pedestrian) portrayed on the sign is forbidden to drive on the road or path where the sign is placed.

So, the sign is round, has a red circle around the edge and portrayes a bycicle. This means that from where the sign is located, bicycles are forbidden to drive that path/road. When you see this sign you need to stop biking on the path where the sign is located and find a new biking path to bike on.
